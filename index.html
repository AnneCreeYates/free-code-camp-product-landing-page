Easy to order


Making a purchase takes just a few simple clicks. Go to the listing of the product you’re interested in, select the size and number of subjects, and proceed to the checkout.

The calendar containing the available completion dates can be viewed on top of the page. Please check if the date you need your drawing by is available before making the purchase.

In case of jewellery it is even easier - just go to the product, select your size (if applicable) listing and take it straight to checkout.
